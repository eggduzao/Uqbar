5ï¸âƒ£ Licensing rule of thumb (important)

For your music + AI project, stick to one of these licenses:
- CC0 -> safest (public domain)
- Royalty-Free / Free for Commercial Use
- Avoid anything requiring attribution in audio output

If the license says:

"Free to use in music productions"
you're good.

If it says:

"Not for redistribution as samples"
that's also fine - you're using, not redistributing raw samples.
